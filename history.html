History

History is the study of past events, particularly in human affairs. It is an umbrella term that relates to past events as well as the discovery, collection, organization, and presentation of information about these events.

Ancient Civilizations

Ancient civilizations such as Mesopotamia, Egypt, Greece, and Rome played a crucial role in shaping modern society. Their contributions to law, governance, art, and technology still influence us today.
