Science

Science is the pursuit of knowledge and understanding of the natural and social world following a systematic methodology based on evidence. Science has led to remarkable discoveries that have shaped human history.

Branches of Science

The major branches of science include physics, chemistry, biology, and earth science. Each field focuses on understanding different aspects of the world and universe we live in.
