Technology

Technology refers to the use of scientific knowledge for practical purposes, especially in industry. From the invention of the wheel to the rise of the internet, technology has continuously transformed human life.

Modern Technology

In the 21st century, we have seen rapid advancements in artificial intelligence, robotics, and biotechnology, shaping industries and altering the way we live and work.
